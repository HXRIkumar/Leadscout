**What it looks like.** The company is actively hiring support / CX / customer-success
roles — throwing humans at rising ticket volume because engineering has no bandwidth
to build self-serve deflection.

**Why it matters.** Every new support hire is recurring cost against a problem a bot
could absorb 30-40% of. The hiring itself is the public tell.

**Wedge.** A RAG deflection bot on their own docs; frame against the loaded cost of
the roles they're adding (A5).
