**What it looks like.** A public help center / knowledge base exists, but repetitive
questions still reach humans — coverage gaps, stale articles, or poor findability.

**Why it matters.** The content to train a deflection bot already exists; the gap is
retrieval + answer generation, not authoring. Fast time-to-value.

**Wedge.** Train the bot on their existing help center; prove deflection on their
real top-20 questions.
