**What it looks like.** A live support widget (Intercom, Zendesk, Gorgias, Help Scout,
Front) is present — humans are fielding chat/email, including the repetitive long tail.

**Why it matters.** The widget is where deflection plugs in; the vendor tells you the
integration surface. High intent, low friction to pilot.

**Wedge.** A bot that answers the repetitive tier inside their existing widget; measure
the deflection rate on real conversations.
