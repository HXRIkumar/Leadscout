**What it looks like.** A recent raise (Form D, press, blog). Budget corroboration
only — never the reason to reach out on its own.

**Why it matters.** Post-raise companies have budget for a pilot and pressure to scale
efficiently. Timing, not thesis (SPEC §2.3 / B6).

**Wedge.** Use as supporting context ("you've just raised and are scaling") behind a
real support-load signal — not as the anchor.
