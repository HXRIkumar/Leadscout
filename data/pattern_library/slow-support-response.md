**What it looks like.** Public complaints (G2, Trustpilot, Reddit, community forum)
about slow or poor support response times.

**Why it matters.** A reputational + churn risk the founder feels acutely. Deflecting
repetitive questions frees humans for the hard ones, cutting response times.

**Wedge.** Position the bot as first-line triage that shrinks the human queue; cite
the public complaint as the anchor (verified quote).
