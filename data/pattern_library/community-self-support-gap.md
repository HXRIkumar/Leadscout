**What it looks like.** A community forum / Discourse is being used as informal,
unscaled support, often with unanswered questions.

**Why it matters.** Signals real support demand the company can't meet 1:1, and a
corpus (forum threads) a bot could learn from.

**Wedge.** A bot trained on docs + resolved forum threads that answers before a human
has to; measure unanswered-question reduction.
