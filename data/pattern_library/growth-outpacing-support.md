**What it looks like.** Rapid customer/user growth language ("fastest growing",
"thousands of customers") against a visibly small team.

**Why it matters.** Support load grows super-linearly with customers; a small team
hits a wall. Corroborating rather than decisive on its own.

**Wedge.** Frame deflection as the way to scale support without scaling headcount.
Pair with a stronger Tier-1 signal before outreach.
